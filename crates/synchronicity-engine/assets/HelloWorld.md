# Welcome to The Synchronicity Engine

You're looking at your **home vault** — a folder on your computer that automatically syncs across all your devices.

## What is this?

The Synchronicity Engine is a sovereign sync engine built on Indra's Network. Your files are encrypted, peer-to-peer, and belong to you. There's no cloud server holding your data — just your devices, talking directly to each other.

## Getting started

This vault lives at the folder path shown at the top of this window. You can:

1. **Open it in Obsidian** — click "Open in Obsidian" above, or point Obsidian at the folder
2. **Add files** — drop any file into the folder and it will sync automatically
3. **Edit files** — use Obsidian, VS Code, or any text editor
4. **Add another device** — install The Synchronicity Engine on another device and sign in with your pass story

Everything you add, edit, or delete in this folder will appear on all your devices within seconds.

## Your pass story

Your account is protected by the story you wrote during setup. That story *is* your identity — the same words on any device will unlock the same vault. Keep it somewhere safe. If you forget your story, your vault cannot be recovered.

For now, it's just you and your files. Make yourself at home.
